# Simple Invoicing Box Die-Line

This folder contains a print-ready die-line for a tuck-end marketing box:

- File: `simple-invoicing-box-dieline.svg`
- Size (finished box): 120 mm (W) x 180 mm (H) x 35 mm (D)
- Flat net width: 325 mm (+ 3 mm bleed each side = 331 mm artboard)
- Net includes: left glue flap, back, side panel, front, side panel, top flap, bottom flap
- Bleed: 3 mm around full artwork
- Recommended safe margin: 5 mm inside panel edges

## Color Guide in SVG

- Red line: cut
- Blue dashed line: fold
- Green dashed line: safe area
- Orange dashed border: bleed boundary

## Print Vendor Handoff

1. Open SVG in Adobe Illustrator, CorelDRAW, or Inkscape.
2. Convert text to outlines before final export if your printer requires it.
3. Keep die lines on a separate spot-color layer if requested by printer.
4. Apply adhesive on the left glue flap and bond it to the inside edge of the right side panel.
5. Export to PDF/X-1a or printer-preferred print PDF profile.
6. Confirm material and finish:
   - Suggested board: 300-350 GSM art card
   - Finish: matte lamination

## Quick Customization

You can edit these directly in the SVG:

- Product title on front and spine (`SIMPLE INVOICING`)
- Feature bullets on front/back
- Colors in gradients (`panelGradient`, `accentGlow`)
- Box size by adjusting panel dimensions in the top comments and related coordinates
